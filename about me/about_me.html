About Me

BORGEAS JULFÉRIQUE MPOMPA

Hello everyone! Well, I'm BORGEAS JULFÉRIQUE MPOMPA, I'm from The Republic of Congo. I'm so happy to go along with each one of you through this wonderful journey. I'm a christian, I'm single and I live with my parents in Brazzaville, and have a very big family with many siblings and cousins. I like and enjoy listening to gospel music, watching videos, reading books and exercising. I also enjoy spending time surrounded by my lovely family. My favorite dish is "Banga Soup with mushrooms and chicken and cassava" and my favorite beverages are our local juices, "Bissap and Tangawisse". But I hate alcohol, cigarette and other types of drugs. May God bless each of you, thanks!
